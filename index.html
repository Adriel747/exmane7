📍 Lima · Peru · ✍️ by Carlos, age 15

Hi! My name is Carlos and I'm 15 years old. I'm from Lima, Peru. I write a blog about healthy habits!

I always have breakfast before school — it's very important! I often eat fruit and vegetables — they're delicious! I never eat fast food at school. My mom sometimes cooks pasta or rice for dinner.

My best friend Ana has different habits. She never eats breakfast! She sometimes eats fast food for lunch — maybe three times a week. But she always plays sports after school. She often goes to bed at 10:00 p.m.

On my blog I ask: 'How often do you eat healthy food? Do you study for tests? Do you go to bed early?' I think good habits are very important. Do you agree?
